MediReach Karnataka

Affordable, Verified & Timely Healthcare Platform

A full-stack healthcare platform designed to help users find hospitals, compare treatment costs, manage health records, and access medical services easily across Karnataka.

🚀 Features
🏥 Hospital Discovery – Find hospitals based on location
💰 Cost Comparison – Compare minimum & maximum treatment costs
🧠 Symptom Checker – AI-powered health assistance
📁 Health Records Management – Store & manage patient data
🚑 Patient Transfers – Track record transfers between hospitals
📅 Appointments System – Book & manage doctor appointments
🔐 Secure Authentication – JWT-based login system
🗺️ Map Integration – Location-based hospital search
